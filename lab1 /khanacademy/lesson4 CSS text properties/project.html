First post-How I got into KBTU

I am so happy to share my story about entering KBTU (Kazakh-British Technical University)! It was a long journey, but it was worth it.In school, I chose Informatics and Mathematics. I love technology and solving problems, so these subjects were perfect for me.I studied very hard for the National Unified Test. In the end, I got 115 points! I was very excited because this score gave me a chance to study at one of the best universities in Kazakhstan.KBTU was my first choice. Second post-A Day in the Life of a KBTU Student

Have you ever wondered what a typical day looks like for an IT student at KBTU? Let me show you!
My day begins early in the morning. I wake up, wash up, and have a quick breakfast. A good cup of coffee is a "must-have" to start my brain and get ready for the day!
Then, I head to our beautiful and historic KBTU building. I really love the atmosphere here; it feels like "Hogwarts for tech people." My favorite part of the day is the Web Development class. We are learning how to build cool websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It is challenging, but very exciting to see my code come to life!
During lunch break, I hang out with my friends in the canteen. We usually eat together and talk about our projects, new startups, or upcoming deadlines. After classes, I don't go home immediately. I spend a couple of hours in the university library to finish my homework and practice coding because it needs a lot of focus.
In the evening, it’s time to chill. I usually walk around Panfilov Street or go home to relax. It’s the perfect way to end the day after "debugging" my life and my code. Being a KBTU student is hard work, but I love every minute of it!
