Recipe #1:Quick Rolton Noodles

  • Time:10-minutes
  • Serves:1
Ingredients Quantity
Rolton noodles 1 pack
Seasoning (Spices) 1 sachet
Boiling water 300-400 ml
Optional: Egg or Sausage 1 piece

Steps:

  1. Open the pack
  2. Break and place
  3. Add spices
  4. Pour water
  5. Cover and wait
  6. Mix and enjoy
